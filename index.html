Who is Eli? Where did Eli come from? Hero or Villain? Join your fellow recruits to uncover the mystery and shape its future.

Project Eli is a hero-driven interactive experience. It enables story lovers to interact with the story and shape its narrative. It's a journey around self-discovery, finding a purpose, facing your fear, and enjoying the trip along the way with friends and company.

Holding a character will give you access to special channels as a storyteller and participate in critical decisions and directions in our journey through a unique process. Whether through decision-making, mini-games, or other interactive experiences, our actions and interactions will decide how the story plays out. Then watch as this story we are building turns into an immersive and interactive journey that we all will experience.

Are you ready to build this story together?

ELI

Eli was created by a mad scientist who was obsessed about control and domination. He was meant to be a killer robot that could aid him to eradicate life at his desire. But for some reason, and only having limited resources to build this robot, ELI was born with a cute exterior. No one would ever believe that he was made to kill. He looks like he cannot hurt a fly.
