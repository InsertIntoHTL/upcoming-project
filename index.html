Who is Eli? Where did Eli come from? Hero or Villain? Join your fellow recruits to uncover the mystery and shape its future.

Project Eli is a hero-driven interactive experience. It enables story lovers to interact with the story and shape its narrative. It's a journey around self-discovery, finding a purpose, facing your fear, and enjoying the trip along the way with friends and company.

Holding a character will give you access to special channels as a storyteller and participate in critical decisions and directions in our journey through a unique process. Whether through decision-making, mini-games, or other interactive experiences, our actions and interactions will decide how the story plays out. Then watch as this story we are building turns into an immersive and interactive journey that we all will experience.

Are you ready to build this story together?

ELI

Eli was created by a mad scientist who was obsessed about control and domination. He was meant to be a killer robot that could aid him to eradicate life at his desire. But for some reason, and only having limited resources to build this robot, ELI was born with a cute exterior. No one would ever believe that he was made to kill. He looks like he cannot hurt a fly.

The majors have landed

After selecting under which major each holders serves, one holder from each community will be voted to represent them. The person voted will be handed the Eli major for three months and will be leading his tropps and shaping the community alongside the team. Each three months a new leader will be selected.

The journey continues

Elis get access to special token gated tools to interact with and shape the story, which is then turned into animated episodes and comic material for the brand, while accumulating reward points.

The marketplace

Holders can redeem their reward points inside our marketplace, where we'll have different rewards catered to the community's interests.

A new dimension

Eli holders will get the chance to upgrade their Elis into a 3D character whcih they can then use in upcoming experiences or inside a metaverse.

Let's play

New tools and minigames will be built in which the outcome will decide the fate of how the story plays out.

Total immersion

The holders will be able to hop into their 3D ELis and play the story that they have been building in an immersive 3D world we will be building.

Expanding the ecosystem

Eli's brand will look to expand both inside and outside of web3, reaching out to the masses and help bring global attention to the brand through partner marketing and having an offline and online presence.
